When the Stars Unfold (Work In Progress)

First Early Prototype of the Visual Novel System in the Prologue of When the Stars Unfold

Started in February 2024 as the game's solo programmer and composer while helping with the brainstorming and creating temporary art assets. Currently When the Stars Unfold is in Early Production & Development.

Description:

A 2D Sci-Fi RPG and Visual Novel game with the goals of a 2.5D exploration and battle mode where your goal is to explore the galaxy to save it from mechatronics that appears 15 years after the galaxy became a dystopia that drastically changed the lives of the protagonists and their party.

Grandmas vs Unhappiness

Game Trailer for Grandmas vs Unhappiness

Note: For gameplay examples only, start the video at the 0:45 timestamp. However, the full video includes both worldbuilding and gameplay overviews.

Description:

Grandmas vs. Unhappiness is a game where you play as Mary Yott and travel with your friends to spread kindness across the world! The game itself was created in a team of 4 with each of us programming different game components and is a 2D pixel-art RPG with elements that uses an isometric-chess-based battle system and takes inspiration from games such as "Pokémon" and "Final Fantasy".
