Grandmas vs. Unhappiness Main OST

Duration:

January 2025 to March 2025

Description:

This soundtrack was created to help create music for the Grandmas vs. Unhappiness video game with an orchestral base to the instrumentation.

Technology Used:

GarageBand, MuseScore, DaVinci Resolve, and YouTube

Challenges and What Was Learned:

In this project, I learned how to turn fragments of melodic ideas into music along with what parts of music composition I should learn more about in the future such as chord progressions and advanced music theory to help in continuously improving my music composition skills over time as a hobby. Additionally, I learned how to use skills that I already have while simultaneously learning new skills in a limited amount of time such as recording a song idea on the clarinet and then taking an hour to transpose the music onto sheet music and develop the melodic fragment to be a full song.
